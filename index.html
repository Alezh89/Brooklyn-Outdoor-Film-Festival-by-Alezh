What and When

August 5th through 8th 2019 our annual Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival will be held in the Brooklyn Bridge Park. As usual we will be happy to see there audiences of all ages. We have plany of wonderfull movies. Check out our schedule and book a place for the whole family and friends. It is going to be fun

This year we were able to book a place in the Brooklyn Bridge Park that can hold up to 5,000 people! This is our record.

The event is free of charge that is why we encourage you to register ASAP.
